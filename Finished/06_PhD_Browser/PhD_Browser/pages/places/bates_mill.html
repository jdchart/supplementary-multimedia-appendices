Bates Mill Blending Shed

Bates Mill Blending Shed (Fairfield Mills, Milford St, Huddersfield HD1 3DX). The first FluCoMa performance "Reactive Flows" occured here the 21st November 2019, as part of the Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival. Doors opened at 9pm, the performers (in order of appearance) were: Owen Green, Olivier Pasquet, Rodrigo Constanzo, Lauren Sarah Hayes and John burton.
